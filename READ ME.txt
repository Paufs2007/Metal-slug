Short description:
A 2D side-scrolling action shooter that resembles Metal Slug, where the player controls a soldier that runs, jumps, 
crouches, and shoots in multiple directions while fighting off enemies through a large scrolling level. Taking damage 
from enemies removes credits. When reaching 0 credits, the player loses. You can also lose by running out of time. The game 
features animated sprites, music, sound effects, a win and lose screen, and finally a start menu, all with the objective 
of defeating enemies, surviving and completing the mission.

Controls:
A and D to move left and right
W and S to aim up and down
SPACE to jump
J to shoot
C to insert credit
M to mute only music
N to unmute only music
ENTER to start (requires credit)
